Subject: Impossible Travel alert for cloud-admin
Summary:
- Cloud admin account logged in from London at 03:12 UTC and Manila at 03:19 UTC within a 7-minute window.
- MFA challenge failed initially but eventually succeeded after additional verification prompts.
- GeoIP lookup indicates both IPs belong to new VMs spun up in different regions.
- Access was granted to a sensitive storage bucket containing internal documents.
Actions:
- Paused the account, reset credentials, performed OAuth audit, and reviewed allowed IP rules.
- Exported audit logs for future threat hunting.
- Notified affected stakeholders and added to priority ticket queue.
