Subject: Suspicious "Payroll Update" message
Summary:
- Employee reported receiving an email urging them to update their payroll preferences via a link hosted on a compromised marketing site.
- Email originated from a newly registered domain that mimics payroll vendor branding but failed SPF checks.
- Link destination resolves to a parked domain with no valid TLS cert and redirects through three HTTP jumps.
- A secure web gateway flagged the link as "phishing" and correlated with other suspicious clicks within the past 48 hours.
Actions:
- Isolated the affected host to prevent lateral movement.
- Captured email headers and exported to evidence folder.
- Submitted indicators to threat intel for enrichment and blocking.
